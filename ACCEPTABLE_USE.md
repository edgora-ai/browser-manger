# Acceptable Use Policy

CloakLite is intended for legitimate browser profile management, QA, localization testing, privacy-preserving personal workflows, authorized business operations, and defensive research.

## Allowed Uses

Examples of acceptable use include:

- Managing your own browser profiles and proxies
- Testing websites you own or are authorized to test
- Cross-border ecommerce operations that comply with platform terms, tax rules, consumer-protection rules, and account policies
- QA automation, localization checks, and workflow automation for authorized accounts
- Security research in a lab, CTF, or explicitly authorized engagement
- Auditing and reproducing your own agent automation runs

## Prohibited Uses

Do not use this project to:

- Commit fraud, payment abuse, chargeback abuse, marketplace manipulation, or fake reviews
- Spam, credential-stuff, scrape private data without authorization, or bypass access controls
- Evade bans, sanctions, rate limits, bot protections, or platform enforcement in violation of applicable rules
- Operate fake identity networks, impersonate others, or misrepresent business relationships
- Target systems, accounts, or users without authorization
- Exfiltrate, sell, or misuse cookies, tokens, credentials, personal data, or confidential information
- Conduct denial-of-service attacks, mass exploitation, or destructive activity

## Dual-Use Features

Multi-profile automation, proxies, browser fingerprints, cookies, and AI agents are dual-use capabilities. The presence of a feature is not permission to violate law, contracts, platform terms, or user privacy.

If you are unsure whether a use case is allowed, do not proceed until you have written authorization and a compliance review appropriate to your jurisdiction and business context.
